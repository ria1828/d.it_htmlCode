Precious Arms is a family-owned business with a mission to create pathways out of homelessness
for individuals struggling with substance abuse, mental health conditions,and/or other life challenges.
Precious Arms provides a safe, affordable, clean-living environment that allows residents’ physiological
needs to be met while working towards reintegrating back into the community.

About Precious Arms, LLC

Who we Serve

                        Precious Arms is a Limited Liability Corporation located in the
                        Tallahassee area, serving males and females ages 18 and above,
                        who are displaced. Precious Arms receives referrals from shelters,
                        mental health facilities, Churches, jails, in the Big Bend region.

Our Goal:

“To make our house your home”.

Precious Arms aims to provide permanent shared housing to
individuals in need along with transitional housing for those
who have stumbled along the way. 

    ✔We recognize that housing is essential.
